Design Choices

Colours
The palette combines professional authority with approachability:

Deep royal blue dominates as the primary brand color (evident in the "Who we are" section banner), conveying trust, stability, and institutional credibility
Magenta/pink accents for interactive elements (search button, "Cymraeg" link) add warmth and accessibility, preventing the design from feeling overly corporate
Multi-color gradient logo (purple to blue tones) suggests inclusivity and modern public service
Clean white backgrounds maintain clarity and readability
Warm, natural tones in the photography (orange, burgundy) humanize the service

Spacing
The layout demonstrates confident restraint:

Generous white space throughout creates a calm, unintimidating environment
Navigation is well-proportioned with clear separation between items
The hero image is given substantial vertical real estate, emphasizing the human-centered approach
Strong horizontal bands (photo, blue banner, content) create clear visual hierarchy
Text blocks appear comfortable rather than cramped, supporting readability for diverse audiences

Tone
The design communicates accessible expertise:

Photography shows diverse, real people in casual outdoor conversation—warm, relatable, non-institutional
The deep blue conveys seriousness and competence without coldness
Clean typography and layout suggest professionalism and clarity
The balance between visual warmth and structural formality mirrors their stated values of empathy, impartiality, and integrity
Overall impression: trustworthy public service that puts people first, not intimidating financial bureaucracy
